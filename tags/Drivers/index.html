SLAM Platform

  • 2023/03/07
  • Comments
  • Drivers, Sencing
I collaborated with other students to build a SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) platform. My role was primarily focused on the hardware aspect of the project. This involved writing ROS drivers for all of the sensors (fisheye camera, depth camera, GNSS, radar, and lidar), developing a circuit board capable of triggering all sensors synchronously, and designing the hardware structure.
