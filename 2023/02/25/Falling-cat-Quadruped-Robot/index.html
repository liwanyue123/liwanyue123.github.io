Falling-cat Quadruped Robot

  • Wanyue Li
  • 2023/02/25
  • Comments
  • Dynamics, Control, Bionics
Category: Categories: Spined quadruped robot

Description:

The falling cat problem inspires me, and I plan to complete my master’s career by developing a creative and challenging robot, a spinal quadruped robot that can flip in the air.
In order to accomplish this goal, not only the hardware and circuit but also the motors are specially customized this time. In the algorithm part, I intend to combine nonlinear MPC and deep reinforcement learning. Now the hardware has completed its design and manufacture, and the simulation preliminarily verified the feasibility of the algorithm.
