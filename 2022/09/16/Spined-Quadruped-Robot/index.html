Spined Quadruped Robot

  • Wanyue Li
  • 2022/09/16
  • Comments
  • Dynamics, Control, Bionics
Category: Categories: Spined quadruped robot

Publication:

Wanyue Li, Zida Zhou, Hui Cheng*, “Dynamic Locomotion of a Quadruped Robot with Active Spine via Model Predictive Control”, IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA 2023)

Description:

I developed a 15 DoFs quadruped robot with three actuated spine joints. The robot can freely change its spinal posture while trotting at high speed. and present a convex MPC approach considering the movements of the spine. The paper has been accepted by ICRA 2023. The link to the video is RAPID Lab.

Responsibilities:

I took on the majority of the project tasks independently, with the exception of the structural design, which was handled by an engineer.
