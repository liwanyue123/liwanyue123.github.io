SLAM Platform

  • 2023/03/20
  • Comments
  • Drivers, Sencing
I collaborated with Peking University students to build a SLAM platform. To address the issue of insufficient or complete loss of GNSS positioning accuracy due to satellite signal blockage and multipath effects when entering urban high-rise areas or underground parking lots, we propose a positioning solution that combines GNSS, fisheye cameras, and binocular cameras.
